Sample Evaluation Rubric

This is the framework I use to keep reviews objective, consistent and guideline-driven.

Accent Match

Checks how closely the speaker matches the target accent and regional sound.

Naturalness

Reviews whether the voice sounds fluent, human, smooth and comfortable.

Pronunciation Detail

Checks speech clarity, rhythm, stress, tone and subtle pronunciation differences.

Sample Task Approach

1. Listen

Play each clip carefully without rushing the first judgment.

2. Compare

Check which recording better matches the target accent and natural rhythm.

3. Score

Apply the rubric consistently and avoid personal bias.

4. Review

Re-check close calls before submitting the final evaluation.
